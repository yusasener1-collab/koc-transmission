Gearboxes

Where torque is required, a reliable solution is essential. Our gearboxes stand out with compact designs, quiet operation, and long service life for every industrial need.

Gearbox Types

We supply a full range of gearbox types to match any industrial requirement. Whether you need high torque at low speed or compact integration with existing motor systems, our portfolio covers it all.

Helical Gearboxes

High efficiency and quiet operation. Ideal for conveyors, mixers, and general industrial machinery.

Worm Gearboxes

Self-locking capability with high reduction ratios. Perfect for lifting equipment and positioning systems.

Bevel Gearboxes

Right-angle power transmission with excellent torque capacity. Suited for heavy-duty applications.

Planetary Gearboxes

Maximum torque density in a compact package. Used in precision robotics, automation, and mobile equipment.

Geared Motors

Space saving, efficiency, and easy installation combined. Our integrated geared motor systems are designed for low-speed, high-torque applications. Many models available from stock for fast delivery.

Industries Served

Our gearboxes serve heavy industry, food processing, packaging, material handling, mining, and general manufacturing. We provide engineering consultancy to ensure you select the optimal gearbox for your application.
