CE Motors — In-House Manufacturing

Our crowned motors are produced entirely in-house under the CE Motors brand. Designed specifically for press brake machines, they combine the precision required for metal bending operations with the durability needed for continuous industrial use.

Key Features

  • 100% domestically manufactured under CE Motors brand
  • Optimized for press brake machine applications
  • High torque output for metal bending operations
  • Precise speed and position control
  • Robust construction for continuous duty cycles
  • Custom configurations available upon request

Applications

Press brake machines, metal forming equipment, bending operations, and other special industrial applications requiring high torque with precise control. Our engineering team can advise on the best configuration for your specific press brake requirements.
